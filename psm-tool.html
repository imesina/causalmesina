Evaluate a Variable

1. Does the variable reflect baseline status or a clinical feature already established when treatment selection occurred?

2. Is the variable part of the mechanism through which treatment may affect the outcome?

3. Is the variable shaped by both treatment selection and downstream clinical events such that adjustment could distort the treatment-outcome relationship?

4. Does the variable influence treatment selection without a plausible independent relationship to outcome?

5. Is the variable a proxy for baseline disease severity or anatomical complexity?

6. Does the variable plausibly influence both treatment selection and outcome?
