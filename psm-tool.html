Guided Variable Screening

This prototype walks investigators through the first two exclusion steps: baseline eligibility and mediation.

Start screening

First define the exposure, outcome, and variable under review. Then begin the guided assessment.

Step 1 of 2

Baseline check

Does the variable reflect baseline status or a clinical feature already established when treatment selection occurred?

If the variable arose only after treatment initiation or was defined using post-treatment information, it should not be included in the propensity score model.

Step 2 of 2

Mediator check

Is the variable part of the mechanism through which treatment may affect the outcome?

If yes, the variable may function as a mediator and should not be included in the propensity score model.
